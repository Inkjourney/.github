# ✒️ InkJourney

**AI-powered storytelling at scale.**

We're building a multi-agent system that automatically generates serialized web novels — from worldbuilding and story structure to chapter-by-chapter production.

### Why

The web novel market is massive, but quality content doesn't scale. Writing is still a craft bound by individual capacity. Great stories depend on the author's skill, stamina, and creative limits.

Supply can't keep up with demand — and adding more human writers doesn't solve the structural problem.

### What

With LLM agents now capable of long-horizon planning, memory management, and creative generation, we believe generating full novels is becoming an engineering problem, not just a creative one.

We're building a multi-agent collaborative system that handles the entire pipeline — narrative planning, chapter generation, consistency checks, and iterative refinement — turning novel writing from a manual craft into a scalable production workflow.

### What's Next

We're starting with Chinese/English web novels and plan to expand into screenwriting, short-form video scripts, and beyond.
